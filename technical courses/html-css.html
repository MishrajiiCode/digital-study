Why is Griha Pravesh Important?

Griha Pravesh ensures that the home is blessed with health, wealth, and prosperity. It is considered to be the ideal way to ward off any evil forces and ensure good fortune in the future.

Important Guidelines

  • Avoid moving in on inauspicious days like Amavasya or eclipse days.
  • Ensure that the home is fully cleaned and prepared before the ritual begins.
  • Invite family members and close friends to join the ceremony for blessings.
